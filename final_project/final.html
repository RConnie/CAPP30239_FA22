Screen Time: Family Media During the Pandemic

Generation Aplpha is defined by young people born from 2010 to present. They are a generation that has access to more media options than any other generation before them. In 2020, at the start of the COVID pandemic, many families with kids under 12 entered a year of lockdown with options of engagement limited to what was found inside of people's homes.

This data project analyzes what media is available to families of young children on the top US streaming apps (Netflix, Disney +, Hulu, and Amazon Prime) and takes a look at media consumed during the pandemic. This analysis hopes to shed some insight into what media is available to Gen Alpha families and what are some consumption trends.
